Shop List is like a ToDo app, in which you can add your shopping items. It is not necessary, that you want to add only shopping items, you can add anything.

Privacy Policy

We do not collect any kind of information from you. All your details and items are kept inside your mobile device only. We do not collect your location and call information. The app uses your GPS hardware for your mobile location. It uses your mobile storage to save the Map Location Snapshot, so then you can share it. To add items via your voice, it uses your internal microphone to convert speech to text.

Location - for map, it uses an external API which is provided by Google Cloud.
